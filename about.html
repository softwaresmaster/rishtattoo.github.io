Our Story

Rish Tattoo & Ramo Mehandi Art specializes in the art of personal expression, from timeless tattoos to intricate mehndi designs. Our talented artists bring your story to life through ink and henna, blending tradition and creativity in every piece.

With a passion for artistry and a dedication to client satisfaction, we ensure each design is unique and meaningful, crafted with precision and care.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small studio to one of Hyderabad's most sought-after destinations for body art, serving hundreds of satisfied clients with our commitment to quality and hygiene.

Our Values

Passion

We're driven by our love for art and the joy of transforming our clients' visions into reality.

Quality

Only premium inks, henna, and equipment are used to ensure lasting, beautiful results.

Hygiene

Sterilized tools, disposable needles, and a clean environment are our top priorities.
