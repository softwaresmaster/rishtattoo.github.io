Frequently Asked Questions

You can book an appointment by calling us at +91 77022 86972, messaging us on WhatsApp, or filling out the contact form on this page. We recommend booking at least a week in advance for tattoo appointments.

We maintain the highest hygiene standards. All needles are single-use and disposed of after each client. Our equipment is sterilized in medical-grade autoclaves, and our artists follow strict hygiene protocols including wearing gloves and using antiseptic solutions.

Tattoo prices vary based on size, complexity, and placement. Small tattoos start at ₹2000, while larger custom pieces are priced by the hour (₹3000/hour). For an accurate quote, please visit us for a consultation with one of our artists.

Rish Tattoo & Ramo Mehandi Art specializes in the art of personal expression, from timeless tattoos to intricate mehndi designs.
